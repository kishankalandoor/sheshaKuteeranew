Overview

Kukke Shree Subrahmanya temple is located in the village of Subrahmanya of Sullia Taluk in Dakshina Kannada District, Karnataka State. The main deity, Lord Karthikeya is worshipped as Sri Subrahmanya, the lord of all serpents. Kukke Subrahmanya is the abode of Nagas. Here pooja is offered to lord Subrahmanya merged with serpent king Vasuki. Hence, believed to be the ultimate solace for salvation from any sort of Naga Doshas.
