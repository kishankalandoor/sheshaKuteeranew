About Our Family Room

Our Family Room is thoughtfully designed to accommodate families with children, offering spacious accommodation and child-friendly amenities. The room features extra bedding options and a dedicated play area to keep little ones entertained while parents relax.

With comfortable sleeping arrangements for the whole family, modern amenities, and safety features designed with children in mind, our Family Room ensures a memorable and stress-free stay for families of all sizes. Choose between our air-conditioned and non-air-conditioned options based on your preferences and budget.

The room includes child-safe furniture, extra storage space for family belongings, and easy access to family-friendly facilities throughout the resort. Create lasting memories with your loved ones in our comfortable and welcoming Family Room.
