Our Story

Where exceptional hospitality meets nature's tranquility, creating unforgettable experiences in the heart of Kukke Subramanya

"We welcome you with the timeless Indian tradition of Atithi Devo Bhava - the guest is God"

A Legacy of Excellence

Nestled in the serene embrace of Kukke Subramanya, Shesha Kuteera stands as a beacon of luxury and comfort. Our journey began with a simple vision: to create a sanctuary where modern luxury meets traditional Indian hospitality. Located opposite the sacred Maha Ganapati Temple and just 500 meters from the revered Kukke Subramanya Temple, we offer our guests not just accommodation, but a spiritual and cultural experience.

Every corner of our resort reflects our commitment to excellence, from our meticulously designed rooms to our world-class amenities. We believe that every guest deserves to be treated with the utmost respect and care, embodying the ancient Indian philosophy of "Atithi Devo Bhava" - treating guests as divine beings.
